Sound Recording

Field recordings captured with microphones from various locations, available for use in creative works.

About

Sound Blue's Sound Recording project captures high-quality sounds from everyday life and makes them available for use in creative works.

Recording Types

  • Nature ambience (rain, wind, birds, etc.)
  • Urban ambience (streets, cafes, public transit, etc.)
  • Ambience and background sounds
  • Sound effects and foley
